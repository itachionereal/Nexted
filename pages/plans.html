Common Questions

Get clear answers to your questions so you can focus entirely on your academic success.

What is included in the "Verified Course & TD Repositories"?

The Core plan gives you access to a curated library of courses and Tutorial Directed (TD) exercises for key modules. These are verified for accuracy to ensure you are studying the right material for your exams.

How does the "1-on-1 Alumni Mentorship" in the Max plan work?

You are paired with a successful alumnus from a top engineering school (like ENSA or EMI). They provide personalized guidance, review your progress, and help you navigate the specific challenges of engineering entrance exams.

Can I upgrade from Core to Pro later?

Yes, absolutely. Many students start with Nexted Core to secure their semester grades and upgrade to Nexted Pro when they are ready to apply for internships and need the CV templates and workshops.

What kind of "Mini-Projects" are reviewed in the Pro plan?

We focus on technical projects relevant to your major (e.g., a C programming challenge or a Web Design landing page). You submit your code, and our experts provide detailed feedback to improve your clean coding practices.

Are the "Solved Anciens Examens" from recent years?

Yes. Our archive is regularly updated to include recent exams from various faculties. This helps you practice with relevant formats and questions that you are likely to face.

What are the "Concours Entrance Roadmaps"?

Exclusive to Nexted Max, these are strategic study guides designed specifically for competitive entrance exams (Concours d'accès). They break down exactly what topics to prioritize to maximize your chances of admission.

How do the "ATS-Optimized CV Templates" help me?

Many internships use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes. Our templates in the Pro Plan are formatted to pass these filters, ensuring your application actually reaches a human recruiter.

Is the "Exclusive Internship Board" available to everyone?

This feature is reserved for Pro and Max members. We curate a list of high-quality internship opportunities relevant to Bac+2 students, saving you time searching through generic job sites.
